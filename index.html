Frequently asked questions

Is JUGAD free to use?+
Yes — browsing and creating an account is completely free. Some software listed is free to download, other software has a one-time price set by its developer.
Can I publish my own software?+
Yes. Create an account and reach out through the admin — in this demo, publishing is handled from the admin panel, but the idea is any developer can list their tools.
What happens after I buy something?+
It's added instantly to "My Library" in your dashboard, where you can re-download it or any future version updates any time.
Do you offer refunds?+
Refund policy is set per deployment — check the contact/support email in the footer for how a specific store handles it.
